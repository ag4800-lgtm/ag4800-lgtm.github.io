## 1. Executive Summary

The Language Blueprint LLC proposes the development of a custom AI-enhanced learning tool for multilingual learners and Students with Limited or Interrupted Formal Education (SLIFE), integrating applied linguistics, adaptive learning design, and human-centered AI to give districts actionable insights, targeted instructional recommendations, and equitable learning pathways.

The goal is a district-aligned, FERPA-compliant tool, co-designed with district stakeholders for cultural responsiveness, usability, and alignment with curriculum, assessment, and MTSS frameworks.

## 2. Core Objectives & Key Features

**Objectives:** a scalable, research-driven AI tool for multilingual and SLIFE learners; adaptive diagnostics identifying language, literacy, and background-knowledge gaps; individualized learning plans and instructional recommendations; dashboards for teachers, administrators, and families; full FERPA and data-privacy compliance.

**Key Features:** AI-powered reading and language diagnostics · teacher dashboard with actionable insights · student learning pathways and progress tracking · multilingual and SLIFE-specific supports · integration with district curriculum and MTSS systems · secure cloud-based infrastructure.

## 4. Technical Approach

- **Architecture:** cloud-based (Google Cloud Run or equivalent), secure API endpoints, modular design for future expansion
- **Data:** FERPA-compliant storage, encryption in transit and at rest, role-based access controls
- **AI Components:** natural language processing for reading diagnostics, machine learning for progress prediction, human-centered design ensuring transparency and educator control
- **Interface:** web-based dashboard on district devices, with teacher/administrator/student views and multilingual accessibility options
