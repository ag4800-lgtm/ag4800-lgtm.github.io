# Data Processing Addendum (DPA)

> **Have this reviewed by a licensed NY attorney**, particularly if you'll be handling student records covered by FERPA. See `00-READ-ME-FIRST.md`.

This DPA forms part of the Consulting Agreement between Consultant and Client.

## 1. Definitions

"Personal Data" means any information relating to an identified or identifiable individual. "Processing" means any operation performed on Personal Data.

## 2. Compliance

Consultant shall process Personal Data in compliance with applicable federal and state privacy laws, including FERPA where applicable.

## 3. Purpose Limitation

Consultant shall process Personal Data solely for the purpose of delivering services under the Consulting Agreement.

## 4. Security Measures

Consultant shall implement administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect Personal Data.

## 5. Subprocessors

Consultant may engage subprocessors (e.g., AI-supported tools) provided such subprocessors are bound by obligations no less protective than those in this DPA.

## 6. Data Subject Rights

Consultant shall assist Client in responding to requests from individuals exercising their rights under applicable law.

## 7. Return or Deletion

Upon termination, Consultant shall return or securely delete Personal Data unless retention is required by law.
