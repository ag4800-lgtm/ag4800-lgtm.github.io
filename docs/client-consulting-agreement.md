# Client Consulting Agreement

> **Have this reviewed by a licensed NY attorney before sending to clients.** See `00-READ-ME-FIRST.md`. Pay particular attention to Section 7 (Limitation of Liability) — whether that liability cap is appropriate depends on your insurance coverage.

This Consulting Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into between **The Language Blueprint LLC** ("Consultant") and **[Client Name]** ("Client").

## 1. Scope of Services

Consultant shall provide educational consulting, instructional design, diagnostics, professional development, AI-supported learning services, or other services as described in **Exhibit A**.

## 2. Compensation

Client shall pay Consultant the fees set forth in **Exhibit B**. Payment terms are Net 30 unless otherwise stated.

## 3. Term and Termination

Either party may terminate this Agreement upon 14 days' written notice. Client shall pay for all services rendered up to the termination date.

## 4. Confidentiality

Consultant shall maintain the confidentiality of all Client information and shall not disclose such information except as required by law.

## 5. Data Protection

Consultant shall process Client data only in accordance with the Data Processing Addendum (DPA) attached as **Exhibit C** (see `data-processing-addendum.md`).

## 6. Intellectual Property

Consultant retains ownership of all methodologies, templates, and instructional materials created prior to or during the engagement, except where expressly assigned.

## 7. Limitation of Liability

Consultant's liability shall not exceed the total fees paid under this Agreement.

## 8. Governing Law

This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the State of New York.
