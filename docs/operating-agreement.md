# Operating Agreement of The Language Blueprint LLC

*A New York Limited Liability Company — Single-Member*

> **Have this reviewed by a licensed NY attorney before relying on it.** See `00-READ-ME-FIRST.md`.

This Operating Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into as of July 16, 2026, by Anthony Joel Guzmán ("Member"), as the sole member of The Language Blueprint LLC, a New York limited liability company (the "Company").

## Article I — Formation

**1.1 Formation.** The Company was formed as a New York limited liability company pursuant to the filing of Articles of Organization with the New York Department of State on July 16, 2026, in accordance with Section 203 of the New York Limited Liability Company Law ("LLC Law").

**1.2 Name.** The name of the Company is The Language Blueprint LLC.

**1.3 Registered Agent and Office.** The Secretary of State of the State of New York is designated as agent for service of process. The mailing address for such process is: The Language Blueprint LLC, 77 Greencrest Drive, Middletown, NY 10941.

**1.4 Principal Office.** The principal office of the Company shall be located at 77 Greencrest Drive, Middletown, NY 10941, or at such other place as the Member may determine.

## Article II — Purpose

**2.1 Purpose.** The purpose of the Company is to engage in any lawful act or activity for which limited liability companies may be formed under the LLC Law, including but not limited to educational consulting, instructional design, AI-enhanced learning services, professional development, and related activities.

## Article III — Member and Capital

**3.1 Sole Member.** The Member is the sole member of the Company and holds 100% of the membership interests.

**3.2 Capital Contributions.** The Member has contributed such capital as reflected in the Company's records. No additional contributions are required.

**3.3 Additional Contributions.** The Member may, but is not required to, make additional capital contributions.

## Article IV — Management

**4.1 Management by Member.** The Company is managed exclusively by the Member. The Member has full authority to act on behalf of the Company.

**4.2 Powers of the Member.** The Member may take any action permitted under the LLC Law, including entering contracts, hiring contractors, acquiring insurance, and executing legal documents.

## Article V — Allocations and Distributions

**5.1 Profits and Losses.** All profits and losses shall be allocated entirely to the Member.

**5.2 Distributions.** Distributions shall be made at the discretion of the Member.

## Article VI — Books, Records, and Taxes

**6.1 Books and Records.** The Company shall maintain complete and accurate books and records at its principal office.

**6.2 Tax Classification.** The Company shall be treated as a disregarded entity for federal and state tax purposes unless the Member elects otherwise.

## Article VII — Indemnification

**7.1 Indemnification.** The Company shall indemnify the Member to the fullest extent permitted by the LLC Law.

## Article VIII — Dissolution

**8.1 Dissolution.** The Company shall dissolve upon: (a) the written decision of the Member; (b) any event requiring dissolution under the LLC Law.

**8.2 Winding Up.** Upon dissolution, the Member shall wind up the Company's affairs and distribute remaining assets.

## Article IX — Amendments

**9.1 Amendments.** This Agreement may be amended only by written instrument executed by the Member.
