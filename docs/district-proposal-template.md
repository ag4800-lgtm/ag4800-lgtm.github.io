## Scope of Work

The Language Blueprint LLC will partner with the District to strengthen outcomes for multilingual learners, with a focus on Students with Limited or Interrupted Formal Education (SLIFE). The engagement includes a comprehensive SLIFE program audit, curriculum redesign aligned to the District's goals and New York State standards, targeted professional development for staff, and a practical roadmap for responsible AI integration in language and literacy instruction.

## Deliverables

- Comprehensive SLIFE program audit report with findings and prioritized recommendations
- Curriculum redesign aligned to District goals and NYS standards
- Professional development: two (2) half-day sessions for instructional staff
- AI integration roadmap tailored to the District's student population and data-privacy obligations
- Pilot implementation plan with timeline, roles, and success criteria
- Evaluation report summarizing pilot outcomes and next-phase recommendations
