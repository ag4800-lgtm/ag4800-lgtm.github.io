# Insurance + Contract Readiness Checklist

## Insurance

- [ ] General Liability
- [ ] Professional Liability (E&O)
- [ ] Optional: Cyber Liability (recommended for AI-supported services)

## Contracts

- [ ] Consulting Agreement (`client-consulting-agreement.md`)
- [ ] Data Processing Addendum / DPA (`data-processing-addendum.md`)
- [ ] Privacy Policy (published on website)
- [ ] Terms of Service (published on website)

Get quotes for General Liability + Professional Liability (E&O) before accepting your first paying client — most consulting engagement contracts (including the one in this folder) assume that coverage exists. Cyber Liability is worth pricing out given the AI-supported tools and student data involved.
