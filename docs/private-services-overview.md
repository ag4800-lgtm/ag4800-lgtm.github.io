## 1. Individual & Family Services

These three services target different needs — read the "Best for" line to find the right fit. Any of them can be delivered as a structured **12-Week Learning Trajectory** (see below) if you want a defined program rather than open-ended sessions.

**English Language Tutoring**
Builds overall English proficiency across all four domains — speaking, listening, reading, and writing — using culturally responsive strategies and adaptive learning tools.
*Best for: newcomers and beginner/intermediate learners building foundational English.*

**Reading & Writing Intervention**
Targeted, diagnostic-driven support for a specific literacy gap — decoding, fluency, comprehension, or academic writing.
*Best for: learners who already have some English proficiency but need focused remediation in a specific literacy skill, including SLIFE students.*

**Academic English Coaching**
Support with the language demands of actual schoolwork — assignments, content-area vocabulary, study skills — for a student already enrolled in mainstream coursework.
*Best for: students who need help keeping up in class, not building English from the ground up.*

**12-Week Learning Trajectory (format, not a separate service)**
A structured 12-week container for any service above: initial diagnostic, weekly sessions, biweekly progress reviews, and a final report.
*Best for: families who want a defined program with measurable milestones rather than ongoing open-ended sessions.*

## 2. Educator & Professional Services

These three differ by relationship type: an ongoing coaching relationship, a one-time training, or a delivered work product.

**One-on-One Instructional Coaching**
An ongoing coaching relationship for an individual educator — reflective practice, feedback cycles, and skill-building over multiple sessions.
*Best for: a teacher who wants sustained support growing their practice with multilingual instruction, SLIFE strategies, or AI-enhanced teaching.*

**Micro-PD Sessions**
A short, focused, one-time training session on a specific topic for an individual or small team.
*Best for: a single workshop on culturally responsive teaching, AI literacy, adaptive learning, or multilingual pedagogy — no ongoing commitment.*

**Curriculum & Lesson Design Support**
A done-for-you deliverable: we design or adapt the lessons, units, or assessments and hand them to you ready to use.
*Best for: educators who need materials built, not coaching on how to build them.*
