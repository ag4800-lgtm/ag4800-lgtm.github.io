About The Language Blueprint

The Language Blueprint LLC is founded by Anthony J. Guzmán, an educator, applied linguist, and AI-driven learning designer committed to equitable, culturally responsive instruction for multilingual learners and SLIFE students.

We combine research-based pedagogy with adaptive technology to accelerate literacy, language development, and instructional capacity — for individual students, teachers, and entire districts.
